Valorant FAQ

Questions, answered

Is Insights safe to use with Valorant and Riot Vanguard?+
Yes. Insights records from outside the game with no injection and no code running in game memory, so it works safely alongside Riot Vanguard and won't put your account at risk.
Can I get banned for using Insights in Valorant?+
No. Insights is a passive screen recorder that doesn't read or modify game memory and gives no competitive advantage, so it doesn't violate Valorant's terms of service.
Does recording lower my FPS in Valorant?+
No. Insights uses hardware-accelerated capture that runs on your GPU's dedicated encoder, so your Valorant framerate stays exactly the same.
How do I earn Valorant Points and Visa gift cards?+
You earn Shards automatically while playing Valorant and by completing challenges. Redeem your Shards for Valorant Points top-ups, Visa and Amazon gift cards, Steam credit, and gaming gear.
Is Insights free for Valorant players?+
Yes — Insights is completely free to download and use. There's nothing to buy; you earn rewards just by recording and playing.
