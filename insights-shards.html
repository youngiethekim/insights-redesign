Good to know

Shards, answered

Do Shards cost anything?+
No. Insights is free and you earn Shards just by playing. There's nothing to buy — the rewards are funded by the program.
Do Shards expire?+
Common Shards are yours to keep. Some seasonal and event Shards are time-limited — we'll always tell you before a season resets.
How random are the drops, really?+
Loot drops and chests use published drop rates. Higher ranks and longer sessions improve your odds, and every open is logged to your account history.
Is this pay-to-win or gambling?+
Neither. You never spend money to earn or open anything — Shards come from playtime and challenges, and rewards are redeemed, not wagered.
